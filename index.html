my design philosophy

I think outside the box.

I practice divergent thinking and start
projects with a blank slate. From there, I
inspire users to come up with the answers
on their own, instead of relying on my
intuition..

I advocate inclusive design.

I believe technology creates many
possibilities. My goal is to enforce
inclusive design to ensure that these
possibilities are universally accessible,
not limited to certain groups of people.
