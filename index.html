my design philosophy

I think outside the box.

I practice divergent thinking and start projects with a blank slate. From there, I inspire users to come up with the answers on their own, instead of relying on my intuition..

I also make it a point to explore angles and perspectives outside established parameters, such as researching less popular companies, instead of only major ones.

I value others’ perspectives.

Aside from users’ perspectives, I put equal importance to feedback from teammates and clients, in order to reach untapped insights and ideas. Designer or not, everyone is capable of giving input that could impact the project and steer it in a better direction.

Let's make a difference together!
